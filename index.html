About

Hi, I’m João! I’m a 26 year old game programmer currently studying at Futuregames in Stockholm. Sweden. Although I’m most experienced in gameplay programming, I’m interested in every step of the development process and have worked with tool-making, UI, VFX and animation on previous projects. I believe this cross-discipline understanding has proven beneficial when working with a team.

I’m always eager to learn new things or dive deeper into topics I’m less familiar with. I’m very passionate about math and physics and am quite comfortable applying my theoretical knowledge in more practical ways. I’m also comfortable working with Agile/Scrum teams and have been using Jira extensively this past year. I’m fluent in version control software, both Git and Perforce.
